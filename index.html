What is Innova?

Innova is a groundbreaking  Edutainment  platform  that  offers  a captivating  gamified  learning experience, featuring an  age-appropriate, comprehensive,  and  engaging  financial  literacy curriculum to eliminate financial illiteracy from an early age.

Why Innova?

Equipping the next generations with financial knowledge and skills from a young age sets a strong foundation for their future financial well-being. It helps prevent financial crises and debt traps in adulthood, fosters responsible money management habits, and empowers individuals to make informed financial decisions throughout their lives.

That's why we are committed to bridging this gap by offering a groundbreaking platform that equips future generations with essential money management skills. This will ensure that they have the necessary tools to navigate life's challenges. We are not only addressing present needs, but also laying the foundation for children to become more financially responsible and confidently tackle any financial challenges that come their way.
